Child Safety Standards for ApnaPan: A New Social Media

Last Updated: 10th Feb 2025

Our Commitment to Child Safety

ApnaPan adheres to global child safety laws and best practices to prevent and combat the exploitation of minors. Our safety measures include:

  • Published CSAE Standards: Our standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation are publicly available and compliant with global regulations.
  • Strict Content Moderation: We use automated detection systems and manual review processes to identify and remove harmful content.
  • User Reporting Mechanism: Users can report any content or behavior that violates our child safety policies directly through the app.
  • Verified Account System: We implement verification measures to ensure a safer community and reduce the risk of fake or harmful accounts.
  • Proactive Monitoring: We work with trusted organizations and law enforcement to monitor and prevent the spread of illegal content.

How to Report Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates our child safety policies, please report it using the following methods:

  • In-App Reporting: Click the "Report" button on any profile, post, or message that violates our guidelines. Users can submit reports without leaving the app.
  • Email Support: Contact our child safety team at mominraza.dev@gmail.com.
  • Law Enforcement Coordination: We work closely with national and international law enforcement agencies to ensure prompt action against offenders.

Compliance and Reporting to Authorities

We comply with all relevant laws and regulations regarding child safety and CSAE reporting, including:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Local and national law enforcement agencies
  • Industry safety organizations

Our team is committed to taking immediate action on reports and working with the appropriate authorities to safeguard children online. We follow best practices outlined by the Tech Coalition to combat online CSAE.
